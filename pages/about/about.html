About SWLP

Social Work Leadership Program is an opportunity to take leadership with social work activities, we are looking for like-minded individuals who can contribute in making this world a better place and be change agents. This is specially designed for young volunteers looking to do structured social work activities.

We expect 16-28 hours from our associates on a monthly basis to fulfil their responsibilities in the organisation along with their regular lives as students or working professionals. All the associates underthe program stand a chance to earn yearly leadership awards: Bronze, Silver and Gold.

Message from Founder
